Sponsorship support helps fund the Jungle Star creative ecosystem, including The Great Food Fight, future HTML mini-games, soundtrack and music creation, website development, computer parts, local development testing, creative contractors, youth workshops, game updates, media production, printed materials, and academy programming.

Sponsor recognition, story placement, NPC references, billboard-style placements, game credits, website acknowledgments, event access, and universe integrations are limited and subject to approval. We protect the youth mission, story quality, community values, and creative direction first.

Sponsorship is not equity, ownership, investment, profit share, or a guaranteed tax-deductible donation. Jungle Star is currently building the mission through business, creative, and community support. Formal nonprofit, investment, licensing, or partnership structures may be handled separately in the future with proper legal and financial guidance.
